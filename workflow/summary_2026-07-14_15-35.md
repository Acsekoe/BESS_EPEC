## KEY OPEN ISSUE: price uniqueness under alpha=300 (and worse under fixed demand)

With alpha=300 > peak MC 80, node-hours without shedding have no marginal consumer; when storage is marginal (base at capacity, peaker off) prices are degenerate in [40, 80]-ish bands and the MPEC picks favorable values: 15.9k EUR/day optimism at baseline (3.7%), 3.1k (11.6%) at 50 MW, 6.5-7.8k in the EPEC. At alpha=0 this was 2.2 EUR/day. **Alpha <= 40 restores universal pinning** (some shed at every price >= base MC). The user's new `USE_DEMAND_CURVE = False` (fixed demand) removes the curve entirely — price uniqueness then rests wholly on the reference-settlement layer and belief gaps will grow further. Decision needed on the demand model before the EPEC experiment matrix is rerun.
